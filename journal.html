Life at TSl has been a whirlwind. I have been meeting so many new people and been achieving my goals faster than I ever could imagine.


I remember the day when my uncle offered to pay for half of TSL and I told him that I did not want to put the financial burden on him. I cannot imagine my life if he had listened to such a foolsih request.


On the flip side, I have never been more tired and cynical than right now. Now I understand why programmers are not the most social of creatures and tend to lack that little bit of charisma. It dies fast.
